Remote Hands Service Plans

Ourstack Remote Hands Service provides professional support to customers requiring emergency, planned and routine maintenance. Our data center technicians are on call all day, every day. With Ourstack Remote Hands, customers can respond more quickly to emergencies, reduce travel time to and from the data center, improve uptime, and reduce the cost of infrastructure management and maintenance. With hourly service plans, volume discounts and 30-minute billing increments, customers are able to reduce their cost of infrastructure management.

Typical Activities Performed As A Remote Hands Service

SERVER REBOOTS

Reboots are free under a Service Plan (under 15 minutes).

SERVER REFRESH

Server swap outs are charged in 30-minute increments (under a Service Plan).

RECEIVING PACKAGES

No charge if the package weighs less than 5 lbs. and only a signature is required. Receiving for larger items is charged in 30 minute increments.

SHIPPING PACKAGES

Shipping is charged in 30-minute increments in addition to costs of packing and shipping materials.

STORAGE OF EQUIPMENT

Packages are stored at the site for free for up to 2 weeks unless a receiving ticket is opened. After 2 weeks, additional storage charges apply.

RACK AND STACK

Racking and stacking of equipment based on written instructions are charged in 30-minute increments.

AUDITS

Audits are charged in 30-minutes increments. Typical audits include, tracing cross-connects, port and power audits and equipment location verification.
